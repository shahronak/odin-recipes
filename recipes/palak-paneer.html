Palak Paneer

Description

Palak paneer is a north Indian curry. It is an onion and spinach gravy that has paneer added to it

It is commonly eaten with rice and naan.

Ingredients

  • Spinach - 2 bunches
  • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tablespoon
  • Onions - 2 medium, chopped
  • Ginger - 1 tablespoon, minced
  • Green chiles - 2
  • Tomato - 1 crushed
  • Sour cream - 2 tablespoons
  • Garam Masala - 1 tablespoon
  • Paneer - 2 cups
  • Coriander leaves - Garnish

Steps

  1. Wash spinach and blanch it with boiling water
  2. Blend blanched spinach
  3. Heat oil in a saucepan on medium heat
  4. Add cumin seeds until toasted
  5. Add onions, ginger, and green chiles. Cook until browned
  6. Add blended spinach and crushed tomato
  7. Add salt, garam masala, and sour cream
  8. Cook and stir for 10 minutes
  9. Adjust taste
  10. Garnish with coriander leaves.
