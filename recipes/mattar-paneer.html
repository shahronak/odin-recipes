Mattar Paneer

Description

Mattar paneer is a delicious Indian curry. It marries the aromatics of onion, umami of tomatoes, and creaminess of cashews to make a rich gravy. Paneer, an Indian cheese, is added to the gravy along with peas.

The dish is commonly eaten with Rice and Naan.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil - 3 tablespoons
  • Cumin - 1.5 teaspoons
  • Cloves - 3 pieces
  • Cinnamon - 1 inch stick
  • Raw cashews (unsalted) - 0.5 cups
  • Onions - 2 Medium, Chopped
  • Garlic - 5 cloves
  • Ginger - 1 tablespoon
  • Tomatoes - 16oz can or 5-6 whole tomatoes, Crushed
  • Peas - 1.5 cups cooked
  • Paneer - 1.5 cups cubed
  • Garam Masala - 1.5 teaspoons
  • Chili powder - 0.5 teaspoons, based on spice level
  • Coriander - 0.25 cups
  • (Optional) Sour cream or Cream or Milk - To cut acidity
  • (Optional) Brown Sugar - To cut acidity

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add cashews, cumin, cloves, and cinnamon. Stir until they become aromatic and toast.
  3. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Cook until browned.
  4. Remove cinnamon sticks and blend until the ingredients until they become a paste.
  5. Add ghee to the pan and refry the paste. It should further brown and oil will separate from the paste.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, garam masala, and chili powder. Let it simmer.
  7. Add cream or sugar to cut acidity. Let it simmer
  8. Add peas and paneer. Stir and let it simmer.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves.
